# Development-First Research Policy

Date: 2026-07-08
Controller: Quant-Dispatcher

## Purpose

Prioritize actual project progress and strategy research. Do not expand safety policy work, do not ask for another architecture/gate review, and do not stop for ordinary warnings.

Warnings should be logged and research should continue unless a hard stop is triggered.

## Hard Stops

Stop only for:

1. Secret, credential, key, token, auth, or `.env` access or output.
2. Broker/order/paper/live/auto execution or daily signal push.
3. Product route, readiness, active registry, or production adapter activation.
4. Actionable investment advice or ticket claim.
5. Test-result parameter selection.
6. Non-public, paywalled, auth-required, or anti-abuse-bypassing provider access.

## R21 Development-First Interpretation

R21 must deliver data rows, strategy diagnostics where justified, failure-memory updates, and concrete next experiments.

Do not accept source-review-only completion for materialization lanes.

Ordinary limitations, unavailable fields, partial coverage, provider gaps, weak diagnostics, cost limits, and instability are warnings, not stop conditions, unless they map to a hard stop above.

## Research-Only Boundary

This policy does not authorize actionable output, candidate promotion, readiness promotion, route activation, daily signal push, raw-data migration into controller, active schema/registry activation, broker/order/paper/live/auto execution, or credential output.
